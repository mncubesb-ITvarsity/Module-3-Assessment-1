Pecan, Raisin and cinnamon cookies

Time: 30 mins

Yield: 5 1/2 dozen

Ingredients











Steps

  1. Cream butter in a large mixing bowl; gradually add sugar, beating until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time , beating well after each addition. Add coffee, stirring well.
  2. Combine flour, water, cinnamon, and allspice to a large mixing bowl, stir in raisins and pecans to coat well; add to creamed mixture.
  3. Drop dough by heaping teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart onto greased cookie sheets. Bake at 350 degrees to 8 minutes. Remove from cookie sheets, and cook on wire racks
